Title: Thyroid and Renal Marker Context (Trusted Summary)
Source: Clinical endocrine and nephrology guideline conventions

Thyroid context:
- TSH outside approximately 0.4-4.5 uIU/mL may indicate thyroid regulation risk.
- Follow-up commonly includes repeat TSH with Free T3/Free T4.

Renal context:
- Elevated creatinine may indicate kidney filtration stress risk indication.
- Follow-up may include eGFR and urine albumin testing.

Safety notes:
- Final interpretation should be done by licensed clinicians.
