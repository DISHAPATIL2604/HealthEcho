Title: WHO Diabetes Risk Context (Trusted Summary)
Source: World Health Organization (WHO) diabetes fact pages and guidance

Key risk context:
- Persistent elevated fasting glucose may indicate glucose regulation risk.
- Lifestyle drivers include low activity, high refined carbohydrate intake, obesity, and family-risk background.
- Follow-up markers often include fasting glucose repeat and HbA1c trend monitoring.

Safety notes:
- Use risk wording only.
- Recommend clinician review for sustained abnormal values.
